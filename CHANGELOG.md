### Added
- **Open Source Program**: Added comprehensive `OPEN_SOURCE_PROGRAM.md` strategy.
- **Community Standards**: Added `CONTRIBUTING.md`, `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`, and `SECURITY.md`.
- **GitHub Workflows**:
